PBJ Recipe

Ingredients:
2 slices of fresh, room temperature white sandwich bread
2 TBSP of Smooth and Creamy Peanut Butter
1.5 TBSP of Organic Strawberry Jam

Instructions:
1. Lay the 2 slices of bread side by side.
2. Use a table knife or butter knife and spread the peanut butter evenly on the left slice.
3. Use a clean table knife or butter knife and spread the jam evenly on the the right slice of bread.
4. Stick the two slices of bread together (the jam and peanut butter sides should be in the middle).
5. Eat happily.
